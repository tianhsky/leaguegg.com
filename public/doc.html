* Aggresive Rate is calculated using the following formula
  rate = ( (penta kills * 5^2) +
   ((quadra kills - penta kills) * 4^2) +
   ((triple kills - quadra kills) * 3^2) +
   ((double kills - triple kills) * 2^2) +
   (total kills - (penta)*5 - (quadra - penta)*4 - (triple - quadra)*3 - (double -triple)*2)
  ) / total kills
rate-1 is within range of 1 ~ 5 and is represented with percentage
